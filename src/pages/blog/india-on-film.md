At the end of 2017, my sister Kate and I spent two weeks in Northern India. It was a total last minute trip – we only booked two weeks before we left – but I was desperate to get away after my last semester of uni and the allure of cheap return flights to Jaipur was too much to resist. 

We visited [Jaipur](https://www.ninetyninedays.com.au/blog/jaipur-the-pink-city/), [Agra](https://www.ninetyninedays.com.au/blog/twenty-four-hours-in-agra/), [Udaipur](https://www.ninetyninedays.com.au/blog/udaipur-the-whte-city/), [Chittorgarh](https://www.ninetyninedays.com.au/blog/chittorgarh-fort/) and Pushkar. I have specific guides on each of these places, but Kate’s film (@kaatejackson on insta) was too beautiful not to share.
